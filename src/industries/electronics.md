        <h2>Precision at the atomic level</h2>
        <p>Semiconductor fabrication operates at length scales where a few parts per billion of metal contamination can ruin an entire wafer. Our electronics-grade chemicals are manufactured in ISO Class 5 cleanrooms with sub-ppb metallic purity and particle counts measured in particles per milliliter.</p>
        <p>Every lot is fully characterized by ICP-MS, HPLC, and light-scatter particle counting before release — with complete traceability back to raw material sourcing.</p>
        <ul class="check-list">
          <li>SEMI-grade F1/F2/F3/F4/F5 chemicals</li>
          <li>Metal impurities &lt;0.1 ppb (ICP-MS verified)</li>
          <li>Particles &lt;5 per mL at 0.5 µm (LSC verified)</li>
          <li>ISO Class 5 cleanroom manufacturing</li>
          <li>Dedicated DOT-SP permitted transport</li>
        </ul>

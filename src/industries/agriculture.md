        <h2>Making crop protection work harder</h2>
        <p>A herbicide or fungicide is only as effective as its formulation allows. Our agricultural chemistry portfolio helps formulators maximize active ingredient efficacy while improving environmental profiles and reducing use rates.</p>
        <p>From classical emulsifiable concentrates to advanced nano-dispersions and controlled-release systems, our formulation ingredients support the full spectrum of modern crop protection formulation technology.</p>
        <ul class="check-list">
          <li>Emulsifiers for EC, EW, and ME concentrates</li>
          <li>Adjuvants: spreader-stickers, penetrants, drift reducers</li>
          <li>Humectants and anti-evaporation agents</li>
          <li>Safeners and synergists for herbicide tolerance</li>
          <li>Microencapsulation shell materials (urea-formaldehyde, polyurea)</li>
        </ul>

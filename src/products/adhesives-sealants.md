        <h2>Bond once. Bond forever.</h2>
        <p>Modern manufacturing demands adhesive systems that bond dissimilar substrates, withstand extreme temperatures, and survive harsh chemical environments. Our adhesive and sealant portfolio addresses each of these challenges with chemistry tailored to the application.</p>
        <p>From rapid-cure two-component epoxies to slow-curing structural acrylics, our formulations are engineered for process compatibility as much as end-use performance.</p>
        <ul class="check-list">
          <li>Structural overlap shear &gt;30 MPa</li>
          <li>Temperature resistance from -60°C to +300°C</li>
          <li>Gap-filling formulations up to 25mm</li>
          <li>VOC-free and solvent-free options</li>
          <li>Custom cure profiles available</li>
        </ul>
